    public class BoxIntersect
extends net.imagej.ops.special.function.AbstractBinaryFunctionOp<ValuePair<Tuple3d,Vector3d>,Interval,Optional<ValuePair<DoubleType,DoubleType>>>
implements net.imagej.ops.Contingent
    Solves scalar values (t1, t2) for the parametric equation of a line a0 + v, where a0 is a point and v a directional unit vector. The line enters and exits the interval at the points a0 + t1 v and a0 + t2 v respectively.
