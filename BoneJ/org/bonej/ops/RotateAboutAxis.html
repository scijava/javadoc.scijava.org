    public class RotateAboutAxis
extends net.imagej.ops.special.hybrid.AbstractBinaryHybridCFI1<org.scijava.vecmath.Tuple3d,org.scijava.vecmath.AxisAngle4d,org.scijava.vecmath.Tuple3d>
    Rotates a tuple i.e. a vector or a point around an axis.

    The rotations are done by quaternions so there's no risk of gimbal lock as with rotation matrices.

    Author:
    Richard Domander
