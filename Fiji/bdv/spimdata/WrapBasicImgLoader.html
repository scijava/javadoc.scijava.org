        public static boolean wrapImgLoaderIfNecessary(AbstractSpimData<?> spimData)
        If the image loader of spimData is not a ViewerImgLoader, then replace it with a wrapper that presents it as ViewerImgLoader. However, note that trying to call ViewerSetupImgLoader.getVolatileImage(int, int, ImgLoaderHint...) or ViewerSetupImgLoader.getVolatileImageType() on the wrapper will throw an UnsupportedOperationException.
        Parameters:

        Returns:
