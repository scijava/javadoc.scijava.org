    public class VolatileHierarchyProjector<A extends net.imglib2.Volatile<?>,B extends net.imglib2.type.numeric.NumericType<B>>
extends net.imglib2.ui.AbstractInterruptibleProjector<A,B>
implements VolatileProjector
    VolatileProjector for a hierarchy of Volatile inputs. After each map() call, the projector has a state that signalizes whether all projected pixels were perfect.
    Author:
    Stephan Saalfeld <saalfeld@mpi-cbg.de>, Tobias Pietzsch <tobias.pietzsch@gmail.com>
