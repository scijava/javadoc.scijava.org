      • approximateInverseCoords

        public static double[] approximateInverseCoords(double[] movingCoords,
                                                ij.ImagePlus fixedImp,
                                                double[][] transformation_x,
                                                double[][] transformation_y)
        Approximate the transformed coordinates of a point from the moving image into the fixed image. Notice the raw transform stores for each fixed image position the coordinates of the corresponding moving pixel to fill it in. Therefore the new position of the moving (source) point in fixed (target) space can be approximated by finding the closest point in the transform.
        Parameters:
        movingCoords - point coordinates in moving space
        fixedImp - fixed (source) image
        transformation_x - raw transform x-coordinates
        transformation_y - raw transform y-coordinates
        Returns:
        approximated position of the point after transformation
