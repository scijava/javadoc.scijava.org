        public static final int MODEL_MODIFIED
        Event type indicating that model was modified, by adding, removing or changing the feature of some spots, and/or adding or removing edges in the tracks. Content of the modification can be accessed by getSpots(), getSpotFlag(Spot), getFromFrame(Spot) and getToFrame(Spot), and for the tracks: getEdges() and getEdgeFlag(DefaultWeightedEdge) .
