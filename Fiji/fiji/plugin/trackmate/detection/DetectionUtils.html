                                                                                                 int nDims,
                                                                                                 double[] calibration)
        Creates a laplacian of gaussian (LoG) kernel tuned for blobs with a radius specified using calibrated units. The specified calibration is used to determine the dimensionality of the kernel and to map it on a pixel grid.
        Parameters:
        radius - the blob radius (in image unit).
        nDims - the dimensionality of the desired kernel. Must be 1, 2 or 3.
        calibration - the pixel sizes, specified as double[] array.
        Returns:
        a new image containing the LoG kernel.
