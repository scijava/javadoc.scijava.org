    public class SpotContrastAndSNRAnalyzer<T extends RealType<T>>
extends IndependentSpotFeatureAnalyzer<T>
    This FeatureAnalyzer computes both the Michelson contrast and the SNR for each spot:

    The contrast is defined as C = (I_in - I_out) / (I_in + I_out) where I_in is the mean intensity inside the spot volume (computed from its Spot.RADIUS feature), and I_out is the mean intensity in a ring ranging from its radius to twice its radius.

    The spots's SNR is computed a (I_in - I_out) / std_in where std_in is the standard deviation computed within the spot.

    Important: this analyzer relies on some results provided by the SpotIntensityAnalyzer analyzer. Thus, it must be run after it.
