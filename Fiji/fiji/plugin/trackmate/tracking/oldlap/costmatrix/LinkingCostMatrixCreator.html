    public class LinkingCostMatrixCreator
extends LAPTrackerCostMatrixCreator

    Creates the cost matrix described in Figure 1b in the paper.

    Jaqaman, K. et al. "Robust single-particle tracking in live-cell time-lapse sequences." Nature Methods, 2008.

    The top left quadrant contains the costs to links objects between frames, the bottom left and top right quadrants correspond to alternative costs for linking (allows no links to be made between objects), and the bottom right corner is mathematically required for solving an LAP.

    Nicholas Perry
