      • SpotGroupNode

        public SpotGroupNode(Map<K,org.scijava.vecmath.Point4d> centers,
                     Map<K,org.scijava.vecmath.Color4f> colors)
        Create a new SpotGroupNode with spots at position and with color given in argument.

        The positions are given by a Point4d map. The x, y, z are used to specify the spot center, and the w field its radius. Colors are specified by a Color4f map. The x, y, z are used to specify the R, G and B component, and the w field the spot transparency.

        The arguments are copied on creation, ensuring that are unmodified by this class, and vice-versa.

        Parameters:
        centers -
        colors -

      • SpotGroupNode

        public SpotGroupNode(HashMap<K,org.scijava.vecmath.Point4d> centers,
                     org.scijava.vecmath.Color3f color)
        Create a new SpotGroupNode with spots at position and with color given in argument.

        The positions are given by a Point4d map. The x, y, z are used to specify the spot center, and the w field its radius. The same color is used for all the spots, with a transparency of 0.

        The arguments are copied on creation, ensuring that are unmodified by this class, and vice-versa.

        Parameters:
        centers - a map that maps spot to their centers as Point4d.
        color - the spot color as Color3f.
