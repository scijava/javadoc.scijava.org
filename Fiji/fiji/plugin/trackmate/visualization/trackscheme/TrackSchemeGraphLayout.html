    com.mxgraph.layout.mxIGraphLayout, Benchmark

    public class TrackSchemeGraphLayout
extends com.mxgraph.layout.mxGraphLayout
implements Benchmark
    This mxGraphLayout arranges cells on a graph in lanes corresponding to tracks. It also sets the style of each cell so that they have a coloring depending on the lane they belong to. Each lane's width and color is available to other classes for further exploitation.
