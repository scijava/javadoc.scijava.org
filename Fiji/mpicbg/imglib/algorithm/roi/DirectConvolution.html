    public class DirectConvolution<T extends RealType<T>,R extends RealType<R>,S extends RealType<S>>
extends ROIAlgorithm<T,S>
    DirectConvolution is an ROIAlgorithm designed to do both convolution and cross-correlation by operating on the image and kernel directly, rather than by using such time-saving tricks as FFT.
