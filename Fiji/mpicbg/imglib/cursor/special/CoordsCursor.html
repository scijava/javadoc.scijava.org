Class CoordsCursor<T extends Type<T>>

    Direct Known Subclasses:

    public abstract class CoordsCursor<T extends Type<T>>
extends AbstractSpecialCursor<T>
    Abstract cursor that offers facilities to move the cursor origin (whatever this means for the concrete implementation) by giving physical coordinates, converted using a calibration float[] array. It is intended for concrete cursors than aim at being used as a ROI.
    Author:
    Jean-Yves Tinevez
