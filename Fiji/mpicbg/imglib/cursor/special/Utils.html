      • getXYEllipseBounds

        public static final void getXYEllipseBounds(int a,
                                            int b,
                                            int[] lineBounds)
        Store the half-widths of a X line to scan to fill an ellipse of given axis lengths. The parameter a is the axis half-length in the X direction, and b is the axis half-length in the Y direction.

        The half-widhts will be stored in the array lineBounds, which must be of size equal to at least b+1.

        This is an implementation of the McIlroy's algorithm, adapted freely from http://enchantia.com/software/graphapp/doc/tech/ellipses.html.

        Parameters:
        a - half-length of the ellipse in the X direction
        b - half-length of the ellipse in the Y direction
        lineBounds - will store the half-length of the ellipse lines in the X direction
