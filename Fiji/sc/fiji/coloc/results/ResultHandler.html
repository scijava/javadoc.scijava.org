  • Type Parameters:

    All Known Implementing Classes:
    AnalysisResults, EasyDisplay, PDFWriter, SingleWindowDisplay

    public interface ResultHandler<T extends net.imglib2.type.numeric.RealType<T>>
    A result handler offers different methods to process results of algorithms. Algorithms get passed such a result handler and can let the handler process whatever information they like.
