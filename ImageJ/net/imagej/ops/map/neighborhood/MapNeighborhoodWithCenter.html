    public class MapNeighborhoodWithCenter<I,O>
extends AbstractMapNeighborhood<I,O,RandomAccessibleInterval<I>,IterableInterval<O>,CenterAwareComputerOp<I,O>>
    Evaluates a CenterAwareComputerOp for each Neighborhood on the input RandomAccessibleInterval and sets the value of the corresponding pixel on the output IterableInterval. Similar to DefaultMapNeighborhood, but passes the center pixel to the op as well.
    Author:
    Jonathan Hale (University of Konstanz), Stefan Helfrich (University of Konstanz)
    See Also:
    OpEnvironment.map(IterableInterval, RandomAccessibleInterval, Shape, CenterAwareComputerOp), CenterAwareComputerOp
