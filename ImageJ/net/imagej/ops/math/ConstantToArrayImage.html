Class ConstantToArrayImage

    • net.imagej.ops.math.ConstantToArrayImage

  • public final class ConstantToArrayImage

    Wrapper class for binary math operations between constant values and ArrayImgs.

    Add, subtract, multiply, and divide are implemented for ArrayImgs of ByteType or DoubleType.
