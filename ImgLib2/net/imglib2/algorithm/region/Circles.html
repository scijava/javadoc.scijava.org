        Writes a circle in the target RandomAccessible. The circle is written by incrementing the pixel values by 1 along the circle. The circle is written in a plane in dimensions 0 and 1.
        Type Parameters:
        T - the type of the target image.
        Parameters:
        rai - the random accessible. It is the caller responsibility to ensure it can be accessed everywhere the circle will be iterated.
        center - the circle center. Must be at least of dimension 2. Dimensions 0 and 1 are used to specify the circle center.
        radius - the circle radius.
