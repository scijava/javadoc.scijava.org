    public interface PositionableIterableRegion<T extends BooleanType<T>>
extends IterableRegion<T>, Localizable, Positionable
    An IterableRegion that can be moved around.

    We put interfaces RandomAccessibleInterval<BooleanType>, IterableRegion<BooleanType>, PositionableIterableRegion<BooleanType> into this sequence such that the Regions methods that "add capabilities" (being iterable, positionable) can have appropriate result types.
