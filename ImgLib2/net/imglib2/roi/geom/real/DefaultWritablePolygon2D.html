    public class DefaultWritablePolygon2D
extends AbstractRealInterval
implements WritablePolygon2D
    A DefaultWritablePolygon2D defined by the given vertices. The resulting polygon may contain some edge points, all edge points, or no edge points depending on the specified shape.

    If consistent edge point inclusion/exclusion is needed see OpenWritablePolygon2D or ClosedWritablePolygon2D. These implementations will be less efficient but have consistent edge behavior.

    Author:
    Tobias Pietzsch, Daniel Seebacher, University of Konstanz, Christian Dietz, University of Konstanz
