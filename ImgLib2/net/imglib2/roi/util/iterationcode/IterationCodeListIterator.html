Class IterationCodeListIterator<P extends Positionable & Localizable>

  • java.lang.Object
    • net.imglib2.AbstractEuclideanSpace

    P - type of the position field.
    All Implemented Interfaces:
    EuclideanSpace, Iterator

    public class IterationCodeListIterator<P extends Positionable & Localizable>
extends AbstractEuclideanSpace
implements Iterator
    Iterates a bitmask that is encoded by a list of IterationCodes. (This is used to iterate a LabelRegion which is the union of fragments which are encoded by IterationCodes.) It is constructed with a Positionable Localizable, that represents the position and is moved around while iterating.

    Tobias Pietzsch
