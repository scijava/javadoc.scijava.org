    public abstract class AbstractSimpleRenderer<A extends AffineGet & Concatenable<AffineGet>>
extends AbstractRenderer<A>
    A Renderer that uses multiple threads (if desired) and double-buffering (if desired).

    Double buffering means that two BufferedImages are created. After rendering the first one of them and setting it to the RenderTarget, next time, rendering goes to the second one. Thus, the RenderTarget will always have a complete image. Rendering will not interfere with painting the BufferedImage to the canvas.

      • paint

        public boolean paint(A viewerTransform)
        Description copied from interface: Renderer
        Render to our RenderTarget.

        To do this, transform the source according to the given viewer transform, render it to a BufferedImage, and hand that BufferedImage to the RenderTarget.

        Note that the total transformation to apply to the source is a composition of the source transform (source to global coordinates) and the viewer transform (global to screen).

        Parameters:
        viewerTransform - transforms global to screen coordinates.
        Returns:
        whether rendering was successful.
