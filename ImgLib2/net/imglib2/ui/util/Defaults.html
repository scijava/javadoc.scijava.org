        public static final double[] screenScales
        For the MultiResolutionRenderer: Scale factors from the viewer canvas to screen images of different resolutions. A scale factor of 1 means 1 pixel in the screen image is displayed as 1 pixel on the canvas, a scale factor of 0.5 means 1 pixel in the screen image is displayed as 2 pixel on the canvas, etc.
